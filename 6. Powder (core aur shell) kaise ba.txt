6. Powder (core aur shell) kaise banayen UV–Vis/FTIR ke liye?
6.1. CoMn₂O₄ (core) powder
Co(NO₃)₂·6H₂O + Mn(NO₃)₂·4H₂O + urea aqueous solution se hydrothermal + calcination se spinel CoMn₂O₄ powder ban sakta hai.
​

Typical: 0.29 g Co salt, 0.50 g Mn salt, 0.30 g urea, 60 mL water, 120 °C/12 h hydrothermal; phir collect, wash, dry; 300–400 °C air mein 2–3 h calcination.
​

Ye powder FTIR mein M–O stretching (≈400–700 cm⁻¹) aur UV–Vis mein oxide‑type bandgap/absorption show karega.
​

6.2. CoMn₂O₄@Co–Mn sulfide / MnCo₂S₄‑type powder
Route A – core powder ko sulfidize karna:

Core CoMn₂O₄ powder ko thiourea solution (0.05–0.1 M) mein disperse karke 120 °C par 6–8 h hydrothermal treat karo.
​

Wash + dry; optional N₂ annealing 250–300 °C. Ye sulfidated core@shell powder milega.
​

Route B – direct MnCo₂S₄ powder:

Co/Mn nitrates + thiourea (excess) ka aqueous mixture hydrothermal 160–180 °C, 12–18 h treat karke direct MnCo₂S₄ powder obtain kiya ja sakta hai (literature methods).
​

Ye FTIR mein M–S bands (≈200–500 cm⁻¹) aur UV–Vis mein sulfide‑type absorption edge show karega.
​

7. Urea ka role kya hai (donon oxide aur sulfide syntheses mein)?
Hydrothermal metal oxide/spinel systems mein urea slowly hydrolyze ho kar OH⁻ generate karti hai, jo metal cations ke sath hydroxide/oxide precursor banati hai; calcination se spinel oxide banta hai (NiCo₂O₄, CoMn₂O₄, etc.).
​

Urea ki concentration morphology (size, porosity) aur crystallinity dono pe strong effect daalti hai.
​

Kayi sulfide systems mein urea thiourea ke sath mil kar pH aur complexation ko tune karti hai, lekin S²⁻ primary thiourea/TAA se aata hai.
​

Agar tum apne lab ke available reagents clear likh do (thiourea hai ya sirf Na₂S, CTAB/PVP available hain ya nahi), to ek final exact recipe (grams, mL, time, temperature) likh sakta hoon:

CoMn₂O₄ powder,

sulfidated powder (thiourea se),

electrode on Ni foam – teeno perfectly aligned ek hi system ke liye.
